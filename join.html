Why this matters

Six reasons to choose ownership over freelancing.

Real Benefits

Your SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and HMO are all covered. No self-remittance, no worrying about gaps in coverage. Just real protection.

Job Security

Membership in the cooperative is protected by law. You can't just be fired — there's a formal process with due process protections.

A Real Voice

You get one vote in every General Assembly — regardless of how much you earn or how long you've been a member. Real democracy.

Surplus Share

When the cooperative earns surplus, members share in it based on contribution. Your work creates value — you should share in it.

Quality Clients

The cooperative finds and vets clients for you. No more cold pitching, no more late payments — we handle the business side.

Skills Growth

10% of all surplus funds an Education and Training Fund — invested in member skills, certifications, and professional development.

How to join

A clear path to becoming a member.

Joining the cooperative is a process, not a click. We take it seriously because membership comes with real ownership and real responsibilities.

i
Step One · 1 week

Apply & Get Assessed

Submit your resume, portfolio, and complete our standardized skills assessment. We review every application carefully and conduct an interview with our Client Relations Officer and one Board member.

ii
Step Two · 2 hours

Attend the Orientation

Before signing anything, every applicant attends an orientation. We walk you through cooperative ownership, your rights and obligations, how surplus works, and how governance happens. This isn't a sales pitch — it's full transparency.

iii
Step Three · 3–6 months

Probationary Period

You're matched with your first client and begin working under the cooperative. Government benefits (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG) start immediately. During this period, we evaluate fit while you experience the cooperative firsthand.

iv
Step Four · Permanent

Full Membership

Upon successful probation, you become a full member-owner. Your voting rights activate. HMO coverage begins. You're eligible for surplus distribution. And your capital contribution begins to accumulate automatically — no upfront payment required.

What guides us

Cooperative principles, lived out in practice.

These aren't just words on a wall. Every decision the cooperative makes is filtered through these seven principles, established by the global cooperative movement.

i. Voluntary & Open Membership Anyone qualified to do the work can join. No discrimination, no gatekeeping.
ii. Democratic Member Control One member, one vote — regardless of income, tenure, or capital contribution.
iii. Member Economic Participation Members share in the surplus. The people who create the value receive a share of it.
iv. Autonomy & Independence We answer to our members — not to investors, shareholders, or outside interests.
v. Education & Training A dedicated fund invests in member skills, certifications, and growth.
vi. Cooperation Among Cooperatives We work alongside other cooperatives — locally and globally — to lift each other.
vii. Concern for Community A portion of every peso of surplus goes back into our community.

We're not building another VA agency. We're building something Filipino VAs have never had — a workplace they own.

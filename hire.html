For Businesses

VAs you can rely on. Backed by real infrastructure.

Hiring a freelance VA means tax risk, ghosting risk, and quality risk. Hiring through us means none of that — and you'll never have to scramble for a replacement again.

No Tax Risk

Full legal compliance — without you doing anything.

When you hire a freelance VA directly, you carry quiet legal risk: misclassification, withholding obligations, cross-border tax exposure. When you hire through Digital Circuitry, all of that lives with us.

You contract with the cooperative. We handle taxes, government remittances, and labor compliance. You get a clean invoice and full peace of mind.

Zero Downtime

Seamless replacement, guaranteed.

Freelance VAs leave, get sick, take other jobs, or simply vanish. When that happens, you're stuck — scrambling to recruit and train all over again.

With Digital Circuitry, if your VA becomes unavailable, we identify a replacement within 3–5 business days. A 2-week transition period lets the outgoing VA brief the new one. You don't pay for the transition.

Quality Assured

Vetted, trained, and continuously developed.

Every member-VA passes a skills assessment, a portfolio review, an interview with our Client Relations Officer, and a 3–6 month probationary period before they're confirmed.

Once a member, they have access to our Education and Training Fund — meaning they're continuously developing new skills that benefit your business.

One Point of Contact

One partner, not a marketplace of strangers.

You deal with one cooperative — not a different freelancer for every task. Our Client Relations Officer is your point of contact for everything: scoping, scaling, escalations, performance reviews.

That single relationship makes ongoing work simpler, communications cleaner, and growth easier as your VA needs evolve.

How we work together

From first call to ongoing collaboration.

A clear, simple process — designed to get you the right person, fast.

Step 01 · Day 1

Discovery Call

A 30-minute call to understand what you need: tasks, hours, tools, budget, working style. We listen carefully and ask the right questions.

Step 02 · Day 2–5

VA Matching

We present 1–3 member-VAs whose skills and personality fit your needs. You review profiles, choose your preferred match, and meet them.

Step 03 · Day 5–7

Contract & Kickoff

Sign your service agreement with the cooperative. Introduction call. Tool access. Communication channels set up. You're up and running.

Step 04 · Day 30

First Check-in

Our Client Relations Officer checks in with you at the 30-day mark to address any early friction and ensure the relationship is working well.

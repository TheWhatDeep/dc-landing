How it works

From inquiry to first day — a process designed for clarity.

i.

Discovery Call

Tell us what you need, your budget, working hours, and tools. We'll listen carefully and ask the right questions.

ii.

VA Matching

We match you with 1–3 vetted member-VAs whose skills and personality fit your needs. You choose.

iii.

Contract & Kickoff

Sign a service agreement with the cooperative. Introduction call. Tool access. You're up and running.

iv.

30-Day Check-in

Our Client Relations Officer checks in to make sure things are working. We adjust if anything's off.
